About Kridha Education & Samaj Vikas Samiti

Our Mission

To empower communities through quality education and sustainable social development initiatives, fostering a society where every individual has the opportunity to thrive and contribute meaningfully.

Our Vision

We envision a future where education is accessible to all, social inequalities are minimized, and communities are self-reliant and prosperous. We strive to be a catalyst for positive change, creating a ripple effect of empowerment and progress across India.
